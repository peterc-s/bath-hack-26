# Trickle-Down Dragonslayer

> May the might of the few reach the many, eventually.

## Requirements

- Modern Computer
- Apple //e
- 80 Column Expansion Card (?)
- Audio Cable (3.5mm jack)

## Usage

Go to [this site](https://8bitworkshop.com/v3.3.0/?platform=apple2&file=siegegame.c#), paste in [main.c](./main.c) click the dropdown burger menu > share > make cassette audio.

Then, connect the audio cable from your modern computer to the Apple //e audio in. Start the Apple //e and wait for the `]` prompt. Run `LOAD` on the Apple //e and play the audio file on the modern computer. After 4 seconds, you should hear a beep. Wait until the program loads.
